Projects

  • FarsTail dataset

    FarsTail dataset is the first Persian dataset for the Natural Language Inference (NLI) task with more than 10,000 samples. To ensure the quality of FarsTail, the data preparation process is based on well-known international datasets (such as SciTail) and several steps of cleaning and relabeling have been performed on this dataset. Along with the raw Persian samples, we provide the indexed format of FarsTail to be usable for non-Persian researchers as well.
